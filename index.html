The Complete Campus Selection Process

01

Eligibility & Shortlisting

Initial filter based on CGPA (usually 7.0+), active backlogs, and branch criteria.

02

Pre-Placement Talk (PPT)

Company presentation regarding roles, CTC, bond policies, and growth opportunities.

03

Online Assessment

Timed rounds covering Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and Coding problems.

04

Group Discussion (GD)

Filtering stage for communication, leadership, and team collaboration skills.

05

Technical Interview

Deep dive into DSA, DBMS, OS, and your technical projects (like this DTI project!).

06

HR / Managerial Round

Assessment of personality, behavioral traits, and long-term fit within the company.

07

Letter of Intent (LOI)

The final stage—receiving the offer letter and completing document verification.

The Project: Awareness & Assistance

PlacementPath is a Design Thinking & Innovation (DTI) initiative created to solve a critical problem: The "Information Gap" in campus placements.

The Problem

Students often have the technical skills but fail due to a lack of awareness regarding recruitment patterns, eligibility filters, and preparation resources.

Our Solution

A centralized, student-first hub providing verified career roadmaps, structured aptitude syllabi, and direct access to 100+ learning resources.
