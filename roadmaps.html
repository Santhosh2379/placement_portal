B.Tech 4-Year Academic Journey

The semester-wise preparation strategy for placement readiness.

Y1

Foundation & Discovery

Focus on C/C++ or Python. Master Logic Building. Keep CGPA above 8.0. Explore the world of open source.

Y2

Data Structures & Core CS

Priority: DSA (Arrays to Graphs). Learn DBMS, OS, and SQL. Start solving 2 problems daily on LeetCode.

Y3

Specialization & Internships

Pick a Tech Stack. Build 2-3 significant projects. Master Aptitude. Secure a Summer Internship.

Y4

Placements & Polish

Intense Mock Interviews. Company-specific pattern practice. Final Year Project completion. Secure the Offer!
